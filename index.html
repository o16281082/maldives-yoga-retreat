Questions?

Frequently Asked

Is this suitable for complete beginners or non-yogis?

Absolutely. All sessions are multi-level and accessible. This retreat is designed for anyone who wants to experience the Maldives in a meaningful way whether you practice yoga daily or not at all.

Is it suitable for advanced practitioners?

Yes. The Fusion and Minimalist Flow sessions offer depth and challenge for experienced yogis, while remaining accessible to others in the group.

Can I join alone?

Many guests come solo and quickly feel at home within the group. The warmth of the Yogi Nomads community means you'll never feel alone connections form naturally and beautifully.

What about dietary requirements?

All dietary preferences are accommodated including plant-based, pescatarian, gluten-free, and dairy-free. The resort offers multiple dining venues with fresh, tropical ingredients.

Can I extend my stay?

Absolutely - we will handle it for you.

What time does the retreat start/end?

The retreat begins on April 10 afternoon and ends on April 15 afternoon (exact itinerary will be conveyed before your departure). Early Check In & late Check out is subject to hotel's availability.

Can I book my private villa?

Yes, single villa options are available for a supplement of $875.

Do I need a certain level of fitness to join?

Guests should be in generally good health and comfortable with light to moderate physical activity. Our program is designed to be adaptable, and you're always free to participate at your own pace. If you have any medical conditions, injuries, or special considerations, please let us know in advance so we can support you appropriately.

Is travel insurance required?

Yes. Travel insurance is mandatory. All payments are non-refundable and non-transferable. We strongly recommend a policy that includes "Cancel For Any Reason" coverage, purchased within 21 days of your deposit. Insurance may be arranged via insuremytrip.com or your local provider. Yogi Nomads are not liable for cancellations, disruptions, or unforeseen circumstances.

Can you arrange my seaplane transfer to match my flight?

Yes. We coordinate seaplane transfers to align with your arrival and departure flights whenever possible. As part of the retreat group, you benefit from special retreat-only rates and dedicated assistance from Mal to the island.

Can I book sea excursions and spa treatments in advance?

Yes. You will benefit from exclusive retreat rates too. To secure availability and preferred pricing, bookings should be confirmed at least 45 days prior to arrival.

Do I need a visa to enter the Maldives?

Most nationalities receive a free visa on arrival in the Maldives. Requirements may vary depending on your passport. We recommend checking with your local embassy or official government sources prior to travel.

What is IMUGA and do I need to complete it?

IMUGA is the Maldives' official online travel declaration. All travelers are required to complete the IMUGA form within 96 hours before arrival and departure. We will remind you and guide you through the process closer to the retreat dates. https://imuga.immigration.gov.mv/traveller

Will you share a detailed daily program?

Yes. A detailed retreat flow and daily schedule will be shared with confirmed participants closer to the retreat date.

Do I have to join all activities and sessions?

Not at all. The retreat is designed with flexibility in mind. You are welcome to participate fully or take personal time whenever you need. Everything is optional.

Important travel note

While we are happy to guide and support you throughout the process, visa requirements, passport validity, and travel documentation remain the responsibility of each traveler. We strongly recommend confirming all travel requirements well in advance of departure.
